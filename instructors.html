Our Instructors

Our instructors are highly qualified and passionate about teaching traditional karate. With years of experience and dedication to the martial arts, they bring knowledge, expertise, and personalized attention to every class.

Sensei John Davis

5th Degree Black Belt, Chief Instructor

Sensei John has been practicing karate for over 25 years and teaching for 15. A former national champion, he is dedicated to preserving traditional karate while making it accessible to students of all ages and abilities.

Achievements & Certifications:
  • National Kumite Champion (2005-2007)
  • Certified International Instructor
  • Trained in Japan with Master Nakamura
  • Children's Instructor Certification

Sensei Sarah Chen

4th Degree Black Belt, Program Director

Sensei Sarah specializes in teaching children and developing youth programs. Her background in education combines with her martial arts expertise to create engaging, effective classes that build both skills and character.

Achievements & Certifications:
  • Regional Kata Champion (2009-2012)
  • Youth Development Specialist
  • First Aid & CPR Certified
  • M.Ed. in Physical Education

Sensei Michael Rodriguez

3rd Degree Black Belt, Adult Program Lead

Sensei Michael brings his background in athletic training to his karate instruction. He excels at helping adult students develop strength, flexibility, and proper body mechanics while learning traditional techniques.

Achievements & Certifications:
  • Certified Strength & Conditioning Specialist
  • International Referee Certification
  • Adult Fitness Programming Expert
  • Self-Defense Instructor

Our Training Philosophy

At our dojo, we believe in developing the whole person through martial arts training. Our approach combines traditional karate techniques with modern teaching methods to create a comprehensive experience that benefits mind, body, and spirit.

Mental Focus

We teach concentration, awareness, and mental discipline that extends beyond the dojo.

Physical Fitness

Our training develops strength, flexibility, coordination, and overall physical health.

Technical Excellence

We emphasize correct form and effective application of traditional techniques.

Character Development

Respect, discipline, perseverance, and confidence are core values we instill in all students.

Affiliations & Certifications

Our dojo and instructors maintain affiliations with respected karate organizations and hold certifications that ensure quality instruction and authentic traditional training.
