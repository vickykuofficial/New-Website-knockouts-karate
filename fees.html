Additional Information

In addition to our regular membership plans, we offer several add-ons and accommodations to enhance your karate experience.

One-Time Fees

These fees are charged only once when you join or reach specific milestones in your training.

  • Registration Fee: $50 (waived for Premium and Family plans)
  • Belt Testing: $40-$60 (varies by rank, included in Premium and Family plans)
  • Black Belt Testing: $150 (special examination)
  • Uniform Replacement: $45

Special Programs

We offer specialized training options that can be added to any membership plan.

  • Private Lessons: $50 per hour
  • Specialty Workshops: $30-$75 (discounted for Premium and Family plans)
  • Summer Intensive Camp: $299 per week
  • Competition Team: $50 additional per month

Discounts Available

We offer special pricing for certain groups and situations.

  • Family Discount: 10% off for additional family members
  • Military/First Responder Discount: 15% off any plan
  • Student Discount: 10% off with valid ID
  • Prepay Discount: 10% off for annual payment

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a registration or enrollment fee?

Yes, there is a one-time registration fee of $50 for new students, which covers administrative costs and your initial membership materials. This fee is waived for students who sign up for our Premium or Family membership plans.

How often are belt promotions held?

Belt testing is typically held every 3-4 months. Students must meet specific attendance requirements and demonstrate proficiency in their current rank's techniques to be eligible for testing. The testing schedule is posted in the dojo and announced in classes well in advance.

Can I switch membership plans?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your membership plan at any time. Changes to your membership will take effect at the beginning of the next billing cycle. There are no penalties for changing your membership type.

What is your makeup class policy?

If you miss a class, you can attend a makeup class within the same month, subject to availability. Basic members can make up missed classes by attending any class appropriate for their age and rank. Premium and Family plan members have unlimited classes and don't need to worry about makeups.

Do you offer a free trial class?

Yes! We offer a free introductory class for prospective students to experience our teaching style and dojo atmosphere. You can schedule your free class by contacting us through our website, calling the dojo, or stopping by during business hours.
